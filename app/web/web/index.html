About Us

Teens of God anticipates for a change in society through its service in the social sector. We aim to promote educational equality, social justice, create opportunities, and harness the growth and development in every sphere of the lives of those who are underprivileged. We’re not just targeting children, but anyone and everyone who needs our help.
